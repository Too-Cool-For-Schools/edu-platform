# Particle Model: Expansion, Contraction and Density

## Key Concepts

### Diffusion
**Definition:** Gases or liquids mix without anything moving them because the particles are moving around all the time.

- Particles move randomly
- No external force required
- Occurs in gases and liquids
- Driven by kinetic energy of particles

### Brownian Motion
**Definition:** Tiny bits of dust in air or water can be seen jiggling around as they are hit by the moving air or water particles.

- Evidence of particle movement
- Visible under microscope
- Caused by collisions with invisible particles
- Supports kinetic theory

### Expanding and Contracting

**Heating:**
- Materials expand when heated
- Particles move faster
- Particles take up more space
- Increased kinetic energy

**Cooling:**
- Materials contract when cooled
- Particles move slower
- Particles take up less space
- Decreased kinetic energy

### Density Changes

**Formula:**
Density = Mass / Volume



**When material contracts:**
- Same mass in smaller volume
- Density **increases**

**When material expands:**
- Same mass in larger volume
- Density **decreases**

## Real-World Applications

### 1. Thermometers (Photo E)

**How they work:**
- Liquid expands when heated
- Liquid rises up the tube
- Expansion provides temperature reading
- Reservoir contains liquid

**Components:**
- Reservoir of liquid (mercury or alcohol)
- Narrow tube
- Temperature scale

### 2. Train Tracks (Photo D)

**Problem:**
- Train tracks can buckle and twist in very hot weather
- Caused by thermal expansion

**Solution:**
- Gaps left between rail sections
- Allows for expansion
- Prevents buckling

### 3. Bridges (Photo F)

**Expansion Joints:**
- Buildings, bridges and structures change size all the time
- Built with gaps (expansion joints)
- Materials expand without bending or breaking
- Prevents structural damage

## Particle Behavior When Heated

### Solids



**Key Points:**
- Particles vibrate further about fixed positions
- Particles themselves do **not** change size
- Increased vibration = more space needed

## Key Terms

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Diffusion** | Mixing of gases/liquids due to particle movement |
| **Brownian Motion** | Random movement of particles suspended in fluid |
| **Expansion** | Increase in volume when heated |
| **Contraction** | Decrease in volume when cooled |
| **Density** | Mass per unit volume |
| **Expansion Joint** | Gap built into structures to allow for expansion |
